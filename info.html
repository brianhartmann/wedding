Q + A
Where are the ceremony and reception taking place?
The Landing at Estes Park is the venue for our ceremony and our reception. The ceremony will be outside on the lawn, while the reception will be inside the property’s event hall.
What is the deadline to return RSVPs?
We would appreciate your reply by April 15, 2023 (pretty please).
What’s the weather like in Estes Park?
The weather averages in the seventies in June, with a side effect of afternoon showers. If it rains, it rains! Colorado deserves the moisture – even on our wedding day! Umbrellas will be available.
Where do you recommend to stay?
A list of lodging options can be found in the “Travel” page of this website. Estes Park is a small mountain town, so the room block options are scarce. Our alternative lodging suggestions include hotels, motels, and cabins that are unique to the town.
Are kids welcome?
Children ages two and younger are welcome. We’re lucky to have a bunch of amazing kids in our extended families, but our venue isn't big enough to accommodate such a large and lively party. If you have any questions about this, please reach out to us!
Will there be an open bar?
What a silly question . . . obviously :)
Are Ubers and Lyfts available in Estes Park?
Rideshare services aren't very reliable in Estes, so it would be safe to plan on using another form of transportation.
Where should guests park? Is parking free?
Our venue has an attached parking lot with about 30 parking spots. If this lot is full, there is a private parking lot directly across the street that offers spots for only $10.
What other wedding events are there to attend?
All wedding events for the weekend are detailed in the itinerary at the top of this page. For example, we will be at The Barrel the night before the wedding to hang out with anyone who drops by.
